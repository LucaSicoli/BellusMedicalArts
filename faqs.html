Botox is a botulinum toxin which is a protein that was first discovered when it was produced by bacteria that grew in food that was not well preserved. Since then, it has been purified and has a wide range of use in the medical field. The dose used in cosmetic procedures is minute and has no effect on the rest of the body. Botulinum toxins like Botox has been used successfully in non-surgical procedures for many years. Botox and other neurotoxins in its class block certain chemical signals from nerves that cause muscles to contract.
